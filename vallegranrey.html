Valle Gran Rey

A small town on the south-west of the island growing out of a steep ravine and out towards the sea and still maintains its farming and fishing heritage. With neighbourhoods like Charco del Conde, the counts puddle, Lomo del Balo, spine of baa or bleat, Valle Gran Rey, or the valley of the great king has names that could come from "Game of Thrones", but is in the winter time is a peaceful gathering place from hippies from all over europe, who at sunset play drums on the beach.
