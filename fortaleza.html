La Fortaleza

The path up the mountain of La Fortaleza is narrow with steep drops, but as you reach the top you are greeted with wonderful views in all directions. The flat-topped mountain that has had a history for the indigenous Gomeros of being both a ceremonial ground for the god of Orahan and a place of protection as the spanish, first led by Jean de Bethencourt, undertook their conquest of the Canary Islands and the genocide of the indigenous Guanches. La Gomera was the last of the islands being conquered in 1450.
